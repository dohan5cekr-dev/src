## 1. Introduction

AutoClickPro ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android mobile application.

By downloading or using AutoClickPro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us

- **Configuration Data**: When you set up automated click tasks, we store your target time settings and screen coordinates locally on your device. This data remains on your device only.
- **Click History**: We store logs of executed clicks locally. You can delete this data at any time through the app.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use AutoClickPro, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, Android version, screen resolution, and app version
- **Usage Statistics**: Features used, session frequency, and crash reports
- **Advertising ID**: A unique identifier used by Google AdMob for delivering advertisements

### 2.3 Location Information

We may access your approximate location through network-based location services to:
- Display your local timezone information
- Provide accurate NTP time synchronization

**Note**: Location data is processed locally and is **never transmitted to our servers**.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core App Functionality
- Synchronize device time with NTP (Network Time Protocol) servers for precise automation
- Execute automated clicks at specified screen coordinates
- Display floating control button overlay
- Store your preferences and settings locally

### 3.2 Advertising
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- App usage and engagement data
- Location data (for contextual advertising)

To learn more about how Google uses this data, visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

To opt out of personalized advertising: [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

### 3.3 Analytics
We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our app, helping us improve functionality and user experience.

## 4. Permissions We Request

AutoClickPro requires the following Android permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `INTERNET` | Required for NTP time synchronization and displaying ads |
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` | To determine your timezone for accurate time display |
| `ACCESS_SUPERUSER` | Required for root-level click injection and system clock synchronization |
| `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` | To display the floating control button overlay on other apps |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | To run the automation service in the background |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To notify you when automated clicks are executed (Android 13+) |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | To automatically restart the service after device reboot |
| `AD_ID` | Used by Google AdMob for advertising identification |

**Important**: Root access (`ACCESS_SUPERUSER`) is only used when you voluntarily activate the auto-click feature. We do not use this permission to access your personal files, photos, contacts, or any other sensitive data.

## 5. Data Storage

### 5.1 Local Storage
All your personal data is stored **locally on your device**:
- Settings and configurations are stored in SharedPreferences
- Click history is stored in a local database
- No cloud backup of user data by default

### 5.2 Data Retention
- Your configuration data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- Click logs can be deleted by you at any time through the "Clear All Logs" button in the app
- We do not retain any data on our servers

### 5.3 Data Deletion
You can delete all your data by:
- Using the "Clear All Logs" button in the app
- Clearing app data through Android Settings > Apps > AutoClickPro > Clear Data
