# Privacy Policy

**Fake GPS Pro** ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Fake GPS Pro mobile application (the "App").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us
- **Location Data:** The App collects your device's GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) when you set a mock location. You actively input and control the location data used by the App.
- **Saved Locations (Favorites):** If you choose to save favorite locations within the App, those coordinates and address labels are stored locally on your device.
- **Contact Information:** If you contact us directly (e.g., via email or the developer's contact details provided in the App), we may collect the information you provide.

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information:** We may collect certain information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, solely for the purpose of app functionality and debugging.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect basic usage statistics (e.g., app launch frequency) to understand how the App is used and to improve user experience. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain the functionality of the App (e.g., setting and managing mock GPS locations).
- To store your saved favorite locations on your device.
- To notify you about changes to our App or services.
- To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

- To improve the App and develop new features.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only:** All location data and saved favorites are stored **locally on your device** using Android's SharedPreferences. This data is **not transmitted to our servers**.
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not have a cloud backend. Your data stays on your device.
- **Third-Party Services:** The App uses the following third-party services:
  - **Kakao Maps SDK** (provided by Kakao Corp.): Used for displaying maps. Your location data may be processed by Kakao in accordance with Kakao's privacy policy. Please refer to [Kakao's Privacy Policy](https://www.kakao.com/services/privacy) for details.
  - **Google Play Services Location:** Used for device location management.
- **Security:** We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity and security of the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
